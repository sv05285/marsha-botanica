Halo
MSM Bar

Brightening Handmade Luxury Soap

The Halo MSM Bar is our mineral-brightening soap, built around titanium dioxide, fine rice powder, and pearl powder. Herb-infused olive oil anchors the base, alongside a rich blend of shea, babassu, kokum, and cocoa butters. OptiMSM® — the world's purest MSM — is saponified directly into the bar. The fragrance is clean and luminous: petitgrain, cypress, lavender, cedarwood, and clary sage.

GMP Compliant OptiMSM® Organic Ingredients 100g / 3.5oz
